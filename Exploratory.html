Exploratory

The Exploratory Robot is multifunctional, and can perform any task it is programmed to do. There are many places these robots can explore, including planets, caves, oceans, and more. In addition to scouting war zones, they can repair satellites as well. The most common use for exploratory robots and most famous is the robots on mars which are searching for what happened to water.

NASA's Mars Exploration Rover mission was a robotic space mission involving two Mars rovers, Spirit and Opportunity, exploring the planet Mars. It began in 2003 with the launch of the two rovers to explore the Martian surface and geology
